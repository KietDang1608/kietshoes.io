OUR WARRANTY POLICY

1. 12 MONTHS COMMITTED WARRANTY

- Nike's products have 3 months commited, only commit sole and shoelace

- Only commit one time for each product

- The product is not warranted for the second time within 30 days from the date the warranty is completed.

- If our shop violates the commitment (warranty is more than 15 days or has to warranty the product again within 30 days from the previous warranty), Customers can apply the method of Damage, change it immediately or Refund with 50% off fee.

2. CHANGE ANYTHING IMMEDIATELY AND ALWAYS

- If the product is damaged, exchange it (same model, same capacity, same color...) for the main product and exchange for the equivalent of accompanying accessories, specifically:

2.1) If the main product is damaged, change the main product for a new one

- The first month from the date of purchase: free.

- From 2nd to 12th month: charge 10% bill value/month.

- The first 15 days from the date of purchase: free.

- Note: If there is no main product to be exchanged for the Customer, the Policy of Guarantee or Refund will be applied with a 50% discount.

2.2) If the accompanying accessories are damaged, exchange them for accessories with the same function that TGDD/DMX is doing business:

- The included accessories can be exchanged for free within 12 months from the date of purchase of the main product with accessories doing new business with the same quality.

Note: If there are no equivalent accessories or the customer does not like it, the manufacturer's warranty will apply.

2.3) In case the Customer wants to exchange the full box (whole box, full box): in addition to applying the exchange fee in Section 2.1, the Customer will pay an additional fee for the full box equivalent to 20% of the invoice value.

3. REFUND: Applies to defective and non-defective products.

- The first month from the date of purchase: 20% of the invoice value.

- From 2nd to 12th month: charge 10% bill value/month.

- Particularly for accessories with AVA power: apply for a refund in 3 months with the above fee. From the 4th month onwards, no refund will be applied.
